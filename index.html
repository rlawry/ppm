Parts Per Million Explorer

Parts per million (ppm) describes the concentration of one substance in a million parts of a mixture — used for pollutants, toxic gases, and atmospheric CO₂.

Mercury vapor at 1 PPM — OSHA permissible exposure limit (PEL). Chronic exposure damages the nervous system and kidneys. Each red pixel below is 1 part in 1,000,000.
